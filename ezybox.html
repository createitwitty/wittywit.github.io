ezyBox App

 

NTADBM, New Technology Application and Designing Bussiness Model is a module done with IIM-A to design a business and design solution for a company.


We designed a Mobile App and its Business Model for a company EzySolare, for their upcoming product EzyBox, which was a DIY assemble solar kit.

Design Brief

To design the UX for the mobile responsive monitoring system and propose a busines model for this app.
The app was supposed to be a installation guide for the ezyBox. EzyBOx is an upcoming product by EzySolare that aims at providing a DIY solar plant kit to end consumers for residential uses. EzySolare is a known player in solar equipments market. At the time of this project the EzyBox was still a concept The team for this project comprised of students from IIM-A and two designers from NID, together we were able to look at every face of the project.
