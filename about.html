About Me

Hi I am Pranshu Chaudhary from the city of sangam, Allahabad.

I am curently studying New Media Design at NID, Gandhinagar.

Having done my undergard in Mechanical Engineering I have a fascination for materials and making things. I in particular enjoy playing with wood and paper as a material.

I am interested in Physical computing and how adding some simple hardware to the regular objects and then enchanting them with the magical code could blow life into them.

This aspect of coding has always fascinated me and has led to my experiments with HTML, CSS, Javascript among web development kit and Arduino, AVR, Processing and Embedded-C for the purpose of the hardware.

But having realise that technology is only a means to the end and not the end goal itself I have always paid attention more to the why and where the tech is to be used rather than simply thinking of what technology. This approach gives me a hollistic, User Centric approach towards my design were the user is placed at the centre of the design and then the system is designed to cater the particular experience that the user might want rather than trying to force fit the experience of the user with the product.
